OUT OF TIME

Web Design

http://perso-etudiant.u-pem.fr/~jpuech/

Out of time is a travel editorial website. It offers a new approach on travels in abandonned places. Abandoned is meant as in abandoned by humains (ruins or urban buildings) or never populated by humains, a place truely remote. Something that felt common to these places was their intemporality, the way you don't really know in which era you step in and from which era you come from when you visit them. The choice was made to use that in the design and the narration.


I was the graphic designer on this school project : mock-ups, graphic elements and report design were mine to do!

You can access the website here and find our 100 pages report (with the process from start to end, from design to concept) here .

Project made in 2016 with Vanessa BAR and Elise RITOUX (LinkedIn)

Interactive Delaunay (W.I.P)

Interactive installation

Interactive Delaunay is an installation using both sound and visual. It is still a Work In Progress (supervised project in a group of four).
The user enters the mind eye of Delaunay when he steps in our installion borders. We are using a leapmotion to detect the hand movments of the user and after analysing them, our program evolves accordingly, both visually and accoustically. The gestures are mapped with certain graphics and sound responses, that differs depending of the state of mind our installation is. We call these state of mind "dreams" and we have planed several : childish (finished), robotic (in development), epic, sad, psychadelic and maybe more.


I am the artistic director for this project. I am in charge of the design of the dreams, making sure they fit the Delaunays' aesthetic. I also help with the mapping of the gestures and with the development of the graphic features of the program.

The program is developped with OpenFrameWorks (C++). You can find the source code here !

The World of Lonny

OpenGL game

Goal of the project: create a game where the player drives a hovercraft and has to validate checkpoints to win



This project was made in a group of two. We designed the storytelling together and then I was in charge of the development and the other member of the project of the desin

We first developped a story to support our game. Lonny is a small man who got lost on a very far away planet. He has to try and find his spaceship to escape. He has an hovercraft to travel with on a word filled with water, but also rocks he can’t go across, waves that speed him up and seaweeds that slow him down. To complete each level that lead him to his ship, he has to validate all the checkpoints.

Each level is designed to represent an element that explains Lonny story a little bit more.


In addition to the story mode, there is a random mode where checkpoints appear randomly on the map. You have to validate as many checkpoints as possible before the time runs out (1 minute).



Project made in two weeks in 2016 (link to an archive with the game and the report here )
