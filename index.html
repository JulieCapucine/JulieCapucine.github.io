OUT OF TIME

Web Design

http://perso-etudiant.u-pem.fr/~jpuech/

Out of time is a travel editorial website. It offers a new approach on travels in abandonned places. Abandonned is meant as in abandonned by humains (ruins or urban buildings) or never populated by humains, a place truely remote. Something that felt common to these places was their intemporality, the way you don't really know in which era you step in and from which era you come from when you visit them. The choice was made to use that in the design and the narration.


I was the graphic designer on this school project : mock-ups, graphic elements and report design were mine to do!

You can access the website here and find our 100 pages report (with the process from start to end, from design to concept) here .

Project made in 2016 with Vanessa BAR and Elise RITOUX (LinkedIn)

Interactive Delaunay (W.I.P)

Interactive installation

Interactive Delaunay is an installation using both sound and visual. It is still a Work In Progress (supervised project in a group of four).
The user enters the mind eye of Delaunay when he steps in our installion borders. We are using a leapmotion to detect the hand movments of the user and after analysing them, our program evolves accordingly, both visually and accoustically. The gestures are mapped with certain graphics and sound responses, that differs depending of the state of mind our installation is. We call these state of mind "dreams" and we have planed several : childish (finished), robotic (in development), epic, sad, psychadelic and maybe more.


I am the artistic director for this project. I am in charge of the design of the dreams, making sure they fit the Delaunays' aesthetic. I also help with the mapping of the gestures and with the development of the graphic features of the program.

The program is developped with OpenFrameWorks (C++). You can find the source code here !
