ABOUT

Hi & Welcome !


My name is Julie PUECH.

I am what, in digital media, we call a unicorn. I have a multidisciplinary skill set that ranges from graphic design to interactive media development, and my last (and lasting) obsession is Augmented Reality.

My curious mind is what led me to move to Vancouver, in Beautiful British Columbia, Canada from Toulouse, Southern France and start two companies. I am the co-founder of The Impossible Studio, an interactive media studio that creates unique augmented reality experiences for live events. She also am the co-founder of Time Factory, a rapid prototyping lab based on the Design Sprint that works with companies to rapidly convert their pen-and-paper ideas to digital working prototypes.

If I am not working on a project, you am most likely under my tent or on a hiking trail somewhere around the world !
